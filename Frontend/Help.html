Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I book a test drive?

Go to the Test-Drive page, select your preferred car model and dealership location, and fill out the form. A representative will contact you shortly to confirm the appointment.

What documents are required for car financing?

Typically, we require proof of identity (Aadhaar/Passport), proof of address, income documents (salary slips/bank statements), and PAN card. Specific requirements may vary based on the loan amount and model.

Where can I find current offers and discounts?

All our latest seasonal and model-specific offers are listed on the Offers page. We update this section monthly, so check back often!

How can I calculate my EMI?

Visit the EMI-calculate page to use our simple calculator. Input the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to get an instant estimate.
